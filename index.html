Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When using the Extension, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personally Identifiable Information: If you upload your resume, it may contain personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and work history.
  • Website Content: The Extension accesses job descriptions from the web page (e.g., LinkedIn) you are viewing to generate a compatibility score.
  • Extension Usage Data: Actions performed within the Extension (e.g., uploading a resume, analyzing a job posting).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Analyze resumes against job postings to generate compatibility scores.
  • Provide actionable recommendations to improve resume alignment with job descriptions.
  • Enhance user experience and improve the Extension's functionality.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take the security of your data seriously. The following measures are in place:

  • Data is processed securely using industry-standard encryption protocols.
  • No data is shared with third parties beyond the external API used for analysis.
  • Uploaded resumes are not permanently stored; they are used only during the analysis session.

4. Use of Third-Party Services

This Extension integrates with an external API to perform advanced resume analysis. The API processes resume data and job descriptions to return match scores and recommendations. We ensure that this API complies with privacy standards and does not store or misuse your data.

5. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request access to the data processed by the Extension.
  • Deletion: You can delete your data at any time by removing it from the Extension interface.
  • Modification: You can update or re-upload your resume as needed.

6. Data Retention

The Extension does not retain your data. Resume data and job postings are processed in real-time and are discarded immediately after analysis.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Updates will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
